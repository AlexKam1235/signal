[ OFFICIAL RULES ]

1. General

This project is an independent online puzzle experience inspired by cryptography, investigation, and collaborative problem-solving. Participation is free and open to anyone.

2. Participation

No payment is required to participate or to solve any part of the puzzle. All stages are accessible without contribution.

3. Signal Contributions

Any monetary transmission is voluntary and represents a signal contribution, not a purchase, investment, or entry fee.

  • Contributions do not grant advantages
  • Contributions do not unlock hidden information
  • Contributions do not affect puzzle difficulty

4. Signal Pool

All verified contributions form a shared Signal Pool. No portion of the pool is claimed by the project operator.

5. Reward

The observer who completes the final phase first and provides verifiable proof of completion will receive the entire Signal Pool.

6. Verification

Verification of contributions and puzzle completion is performed manually using publicly available blockchain data and timestamps.

7. Uncertainty

There is no guarantee that:

  • a final phase will be reached
  • a winner will be determined
  • the Signal Pool will be redistributed

8. Acceptance

Participation indicates understanding and acceptance of the experimental nature of this project.
